# Task

The **main task of Week 3 is to make your own preprocessing pipeline for the project.**
To make your own preprocessing pipeline do the following:

- Read and understand [Tribev2](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04326), this has a list of all datasets that are relevant in this field. 
- After understanding the datasets, research and build an appropriate preprocessing pipeline for this data. You may refer to [Algonauts 2025](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2501.00504) and [Tribe](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2507.22229) for inspiration. 
- Also look at the other winning solutions of Algonauts 2025 for inspiration regarding your pipeline. 
- You may look at papers that are **not in this field but still use the same datasets/fMRI data for inspiration as well.**


**Final Deliverable: A preprocessing pipeline with all the fMRI data preprocessed.**
